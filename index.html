About Me

Hey there! I’m cyb3ritic, a cyber sleuth in training, currently decoding my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Bangalore. By day, I’m a student; by night, I’m hacking my way through CTFs as part of India’s top team, bi0s. Since joining bi0sblr in 2023, I’ve cracked over 20 CTFs, specializing in web exploitation.

I’ve got a terminal addiction to Linux and handle tools like Nmap, Metasploit, and Burp Suite like a pro. When I’m not scripting in Python or Bash, I’m breaking the web (ethically, of course) with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I share my hacking adventures on blog.samipshah.com.np. Beyond CTFs, I’m into bug bounty hunting.

Always up for a challenge, I’m eager to patch my knowledge gaps and contribute to the cybersecurity world—because who doesn’t love cracking a tough nut?
